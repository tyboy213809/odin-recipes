Bang Bang Sauce

Description

This bang bang sauce is a sweet and spicy mayonnaise-based sauce that is good on fried fish, chicken, fried potatoes — almost everything!

Ingredients

  • Step 1

    • ½ cup mayonnaise

  • Step 2

    • 2 tablespoons sweet Thai chili sauce

  • Step 3

    • 1 tablespoon Sriracha sauce

  • Step 4

    • 1 teaspoon rice vinegar

Directions

  1. step 1

    2. Stir together mayonnaise, chili sauce, Sriracha, and rice vinegar in a small bowl until smooth.
